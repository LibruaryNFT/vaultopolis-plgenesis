# Vaultopolis — Demo Video Script (3-5 minutes)

## Recording Notes
- Screen record vaultopolis.com with voiceover
- Keep it moving — show, don't explain
- Resolution: 1920x1080

---

## Scene 1: Hook (15 seconds)

**Show**: Vaultopolis homepage / landing

**Say**: "Flow has millions of collectors across NBA Top Shot, NFL All Day, and Disney Pinnacle. But there's no single place to see what's happening across these markets. Vaultopolis changes that."

---

## Scene 2: Market Analytics (60 seconds)

**Show**: Navigate to Stats page, show Top Shot tab

**Say**: "Vaultopolis indexes over 611 million blockchain events and delivers 80+ metrics per edition across all three collections."

**Show**: Click through tabs — Top Shot, All Day, Pinnacle. Show edition tables with columns.

**Say**: "Floor prices, sale history, supply breakdowns, liquidity scores, deal detection — everything a collector needs to make informed decisions."

**Show**: Click into an Edition Detail page. Show supply bar, stat cards, price chart.

**Say**: "Every edition has its own analytics page with price history, supply visualization, and buy/sell signals based on ask-to-estimated-value ratios."

**Show**: Market Overview tab — top movers, trend charts.

**Say**: "The Market Overview gives a cross-collection snapshot — top movers, most valuable editions, and volume trends over any timeframe."

---

## Scene 3: Real-Time Feeds (30 seconds)

**Show**: Navigate to live event feed. Show sales, mints, transfers updating.

**Say**: "Live event feeds track every sale, mint, transfer, and burn as it happens on-chain. Filter by event type, player, or tier to watch exactly what matters to you."

---

## Scene 4: Player Rankings (20 seconds)

**Show**: Navigate to Rankings page. Sort by market cap. Filter by team.

**Say**: "Cross-platform player rankings let you compare athletes across all three collections — sorted by market cap, floor price, or sales volume."

---

## Scene 5: Portfolio Intelligence (45 seconds)

**Show**: Connect wallet (or show a public portfolio by address)

**Say**: "Connect your Flow wallet and see your entire portfolio valued instantly — across all collections in one view."

**Show**: Portfolio overview loading fast. Show top 10 most valuable items, collection breakdown.

**Say**: "We optimized portfolio loading from 267 individual blockchain queries down to a single server call. Even portfolios with hundreds of moments load in seconds."

**Show**: Scroll through edition-level breakdown with valuations.

**Say**: "Every moment is valued using our 80+ metric engine, so you always know what your collection is worth."

**Show**: Navigate to a public portfolio URL.

**Say**: "You can also view any wallet's portfolio publicly — just paste the address. No wallet connection required."

---

## Scene 6: Browser Extension (30 seconds)

**Show**: Screen recording of the extension in action on nbatopshot.com

**Say**: "We also built a Chrome extension that brings Vaultopolis analytics directly into marketplace pages."

**Show**: Hover over collectible cards, show tooltip appearing with floor price, supply, tier.

**Say**: "Hover over any moment on NBA Top Shot, NFL All Day, or Disney Pinnacle and instantly see floor price, supply data, and tier — without leaving the page. Market intelligence where collectors already shop."

---

## Scene 7: Architecture (20 seconds)

**Show**: Architecture diagram (can be a slide or overlay)

**Say**: "Under the hood: a continuous Flow blockchain indexer feeding PostgreSQL with TimescaleDB, a refresh engine rebuilding analytics every 6 hours, and APIs serving both our web app and browser extension. All built on Flow-native infrastructure."

---

## Scene 8: Close (15 seconds)

**Show**: Back to homepage or portfolio view

**Say**: "Vaultopolis — the analytics layer Flow's collectibles economy has been waiting for. Live at vaultopolis.com, built for Flow, by Flow builders."

---
